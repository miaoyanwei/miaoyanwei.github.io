Evaluate the checkout flow of Ableton's website for APEC markets

How might we assess, identify issues & discover opportunities to improve Ableton APAC site's checkout flow?

  • Assess current Ableton local webstore in regards to UX and local e-commerce sites best practices.
  • Identify checkout flow best practices for e-commerce sites in key target regions such as China, Japan & South Korea.
  • Identify key recommendations to improve current checkout flow.

Proces Overview

  • We identified heuristics issues on the checkout flow based on Nielsen's 10 Usability Heuristics.
  • Checkout flow covers screens starting from Cart to Order Confirmation, and each screen's various states (i.e. success & error alerts).
  • We reviewed checkout flows for Live, Push, Live + Push bundle, and Live Upgrade, and analyzed the flows for new users & return users.
  • Reviewed best practices from local e-commerce sites and global brands that have localized online shops.
  • Analyzed and synthesized insights into key recommendations.

Insights & Recommendations

Following a thorough analysis, we have generated valuable Insights & Recommendations that primarily concentrate on the following areas: Cart Review, Coupon Application, Help Access, Form Completion, Payment Process, Order Confirmation, Login/Signup, and Localization Copy. Additionally, we also prioritised the recommendations based on their significance, aiming to assist our client in identifying key features for development planning.
