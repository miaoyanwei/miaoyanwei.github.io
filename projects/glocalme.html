The GlocalMe devices are portable WiFi hotspots that allow users to connect to the internet while on the go. The flexibility and convenience of being able to stay connected globally make GlocalMe a sought-after solution for those who need internet access wherever they are.

To enhance the use experience, increase user engagement, and encourage retention, we enabled users to manage the device through the app on their phone. They don't have to dig into their bag to reach the device whenever they want to manage it.

We extract crucial information that matters most to users from the data plans. Utilising data visualisation techniques, we present this information in a clear and understandable manner, aiming to prevent any unnecessary stress for users.

To alleviate waiting anxiety and keep users informed about the VSIM connecting status, we broke down the loading process into distinct stages and displayed them progressively.
