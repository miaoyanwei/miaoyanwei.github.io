Insights to opportunities

Humans are bad passive monitors

To design a system which communicate clearly and quickly trust and security

In L3 - You are taking the primary control away from the human

To ensure the system is ahead of and more intuitive than the user

Keeping the driver aware is one of the main challenges

The driver needs to see only critical information, leave out the rest

Users are discouraged in system too technical, they want to take control back as that feels more familiar

Use heristic principles to help users feel more familiar with real world objects, to enable feeling of control
